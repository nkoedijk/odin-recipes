Air Fryer Potato Wedges

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 1½ tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground paprika
  • ½ teaspoon parsley flakes
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Cut each potato in half lenghtwise. Cut each half in half lenghtwise and then cut each quarter in half lenghtwise. You will have 16 wedges.
  3. Place potato wedges in a large bowl. Add olive oil, paprika, parsley, chili, salt and peper. Mix until well combined.
  4. Place 1/2 of the potato wedges in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Flip wedges with tongs and cook for an aditional 5 minutes. Remove to a plate.
  6. Repeat to cook the remaining wedges.
